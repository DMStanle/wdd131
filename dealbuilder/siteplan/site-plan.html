Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site willl be to create custom deals on items inside of a store. For example, sometimes a store runs promotions where deals need to be a certain percentage off or where an item is free but still needs to be taxed. After the deal constructed, a QR code will be given which can be printed and taken to a cashier who can scan it and know how to charge the customer.

Audience

This site is being made for my existing job at the BYUI University Store for technology salesmen. The site will be used by the salesmen to make the deal and easily print off a QR code with the information needed to properly charge the customer. The information will include an item name, short description of the deal such as (50% off), and dollar ammount to charge the customer. *Important note: The QR code may seem excessive, but the idea is that the cashiers will be using a handheld gun that transfers the information, but for this assignment I will not be going further than making a simple QR code.

Dynamic elements

There will be two dropdown menus. One with a lis of products, and one with a list of deals. When they select the desired product, they can then see the base price, adn when they select a deal, the specifics of that deal will apply to the toal at the bottom. For this assignment, only product and one deal at a time will be possible. Adjustments include percentage discounts, tax exemptions and "free items that are still taxed at full price. Total, a summary of the deal, and the name of the product will be displayed. This will all be out into a QR code that can be printed or screenshotted.

Content

Home page

Deal Builder is a site that enables you as a store emplyee to make a custom deal quickly and effectively. If you want to take 50% a product, you can do it with the click of a button. If you want to waive the tax, no problem! Custom pricing with promotions and simply catering more specifically to unique situations is the purpose of this tool.

Once your deal is created, you can easily generate a QR code with the click of a button and print it off or take a screenshot. When the cashier scans the code, they will see the product, the deal details, and the total dollar ammount to charge the customer. This will help simllify the process and make it easier to maintain accurate communication from the salesman to the cashier while saving time.
